Common Mental Diseases & Their Treatments

Depression

Causes: Stress, trauma, genetics.

Treatment: Therapy, medication, lifestyle changes.

Anxiety

Causes: Stress, chemical imbalances, environment.

Treatment: Meditation, breathing exercises, medication.

Insomnia

Causes: Stress, anxiety, poor sleep habits.

Treatment: Good sleep hygiene, relaxation techniques, medication.

Bipolar Disorder

Causes: Genetic factors, brain structure.

Treatment: Mood stabilizers, therapy, lifestyle management.

Schizophrenia

Causes: Genetics, brain chemistry, environment.

Treatment: Antipsychotic medication, therapy.

OCD

Causes: Genetics, brain function.

Treatment: CBT, medication.

PTSD

Causes: Trauma, extreme stress.

Treatment: Therapy, medication.

ADHD

Causes: Genetics, brain structure.

Treatment: Therapy, medication, routine management.

Common Physical Diseases & Their Cures

Headache

Causes: Stress, dehydration, tension.

Cure: Rest, hydration, medication, stress management.
Common Medicines: Acetaminophen, Ibuprofen,Combiflame,Paracetamol, Aspirin.

Migraines

Causes: Stress, lack of sleep, dietary triggers.

Cure: Adequate sleep, hydration, avoiding triggers, medication.
Common Medicines: Triptans, NSAIDs.

Back Pain

Causes: Poor posture, muscle strain, injury.

Cure: Regular stretching, ergonomic seating, physiotherapy.
Common Medicines: Ibuprofen, Acetaminophen, Muscle Relaxants.

Arthritis

Causes: Inflammation of joints, aging, genetics.

Cure: Anti-inflammatory diet, exercise, medication, physiotherapy.
Common Medicines: NSAIDs, Disease-modifying anti-rheumatic.

Obesity

Causes: Poor diet, lack of exercise, genetics.

Cure: Balanced diet, regular exercise, medical consultation.
Common Medicines: Orlistat, Phentermine.

Stomachache

Causes: Indigestion, infection, gas, constipation.

Cure: Adequate hydration, balanced diet, medication.
Common Medicines: Antacids, Loperamide.

Cough

Causes: Infection, allergies, environmental factors.

Cure: Cough syrups, humidifiers, rest.
Common Medicines: Dextromethorphan,Hriask,Chestoncold, Codeine.

Fever

Causes: Infections, inflammation, body temperature imbalance.

Cure: Hydration, fever-reducing medication, rest.
Common Medicines: Paracetamol, Ibuprofen.

High Blood Pressure

Causes: Stress, genetics, excessive salt intake.

Cure: Healthy diet, exercise, medication.
Common Medicines: ACE inhibitors, Diuretics.
